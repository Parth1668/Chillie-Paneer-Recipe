Paneer chilie Recipe

Chilli Paneer is one of the popular recipe from the Indian Chinese cuisine. There are many ways of making it. You can make it dry, semi dry or a gravy version. In this post I am sharing the semi dry version. .

Preparing batter

Step 1:
In a bowl take 2 tablespoons cornstarch and 3 tablespoons maida (all purpose flour).

Step 2:
Next add ½ teaspoon ginger paste and ½ teaspoon garlic paste.

Step 3:
Now add ¼ teaspoon crushed black pepper or black pepper powder and ¼ teaspoon Kashmiri red chilli powder
. Also season with salt as required.

Step 4:
Then add ¼ cup water or add as required. With a wired whisk or a spoon mix very well to get a smooth batter without any lumps. The batter
is of medium consistency.

Step 5:
Now add paneer cubes in the batter. With a spoon gently coat the paneer cubes with the batter.

Frying Paneer


Step 1:
Heat 3 tablespoons oil in a frying pan. I have pan fried the paneer cubes. You can also deep fry them.

Step 2:
Once one side is done, flip it and fry the other side. Flip a couple of times and fry till the paneer cubes are
crisp and golden. Also do not over do the frying as then the paneer cubes can become hard.

Step 3
Remove the fried paneer cubes and place them on kitchen paper towels for the extra oil to be absorbed.

Stir frying and finalinsing


Step 1
In the same pan, there will be some oil left. So no need to add any extra oil. Add 2 teaspoons finely chopped ginger,
2 teaspoons finely chopped garlic and 2 to 3 green chillies (slit or sliced).

Step 2
Stir fry on medium to high flame for some seconds.

Step 3
Then add ⅓ cup chopped spring onions. Stir fry spring onions for 1 to 2 minutes on medium to high flame.

Step 4
Next add ½ cup sliced capsicum.

Step 5
Stir fry on medium to high flame for 3 to 4 minutes. The capsicum only needs to slightly cooked.

Step 6
Lower the flame and then add ½ teaspoon Kashmiri red chilli powder.

Step 7
Then add 2 teaspoons soy sauce.

Step 8
Next add 1 teaspoon red chilli sauce.

Step 9
Stir and mix the sauces and red chilli powder with the remaining stir fried ingredients.

Step 10
Next add 1 teaspoon red chilli sauce .Stir and mix the sauces and red chilli powder with the remaining stir fried ingredients.

Step 11
Add ½ to ⅔ cup water. Mix well.

Step 12
Let the mixture come to a simmer on medium to medium-high flame.

Step 13
Take 1 tablespoon cornstarch in a small bowl.

Step 14
Add 2 to 3 tablespoons water for dissolving corn starch. Corn starch is known as corn flour in India .

Step 15
Mix very well to get a smooth paste.
Step 16
Lower the flame and then add this corn starch paste to the gravy.

Step 17
As soon as you add, mix the paste very well so that no lumps are formed .

Step 18
Add ½ to 1 teaspoon sugar or as required.

Step 19
Season with salt as per taste. Do keep a check on salt as soy sauce and red chilli sauce already have salt in them.

Step 20
Mix very well and simmer till the sauce has slightly thickened and the corn starch has cooked. You can check the
taste and you should not feel any raw taste coming from the corn starch.

Step 21
Add the fried paneer cubes.

Step 22
Mix paneer cubes very well with the gravy.

Step 23
Switch off the flame and then add ¼ to ½ teaspoon rice vinegar or white vinegar. You can skip vinegar if you want.

Step 24
Then add about 2 to 3 tablespoons of chopped spring onions greens. mix very well. Check the taste and if you want, you can add more soy sauce,
chilli sauce, salt, black pepper or sugar.

Step 25
Serve chilli paneer restaurant style garnished with some spring onions. You can serve it with any Indo Chinese rice recipe.

Your mouth watering Chilli Paneer is done ! Enjoy !
